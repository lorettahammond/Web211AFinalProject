ASSEMBLY INSTRUCTIONS
1. Draw diagonal line on wrong side of green print 3 1/4" square with marking tool of your choice. Referring to Diagram I, place green print square on background 12 1/2" square, right sides together, aligning raw edges. Stitch on drawn line; trim away and discard excess fabric. Repeat at each corner. Press seam toward green print to make pieced square.

2. Referring to Diagram I, position template fabrics (in alphabetical order). Using appliqué method of choice, appliqué in place to complete Primitive Tulip Block.

Quilt Wall Hanging Assembly

Yardage Requirements
Background ... 1 yard
Solid green (leaves, stems, inner border) ... 1/2 yard
Dark Pink Print (bottom of tulip) ... 1/4 yard
Medium pink print ... (top of tulip) ... 1/4 yard
Green print ... (corner squares, outer border, binding) ... 1 3/8 yards
Backing (50" pieced square) ... 2 3/4 yards
Batting (50" square) ... Twin size

Cutting Instructions:
Background
... 9 squares 12 1/2" x 12 1/2"
Solid green
... *4 strips 1 1/2" x WOF (width of fabric)
... 5 Template A
... 10 Template B
Dark pink print
... 5 Template C

Medium pink print
... 5 Template D
Green print
... *4 strips 4 1/2" x LOF (length of fabric ... lengthwise grain)
... *4 strips 2 1/2" x LOF (size for doubled binding)
... 20 squares 3 1/4" x 3 1/4"

Quilt Top Assembly Instructions
Step 1. Refer to Primitive Tulip Block assembly instructions to make 5 appliqué blocks total.
Step 2. Referring to Assembly Diagram, alternate Primitive Tulip Blocks and background 12 1/2" squares, stitch 3 rows. Sew rows together.
Step 3. Sew solid green 1 1/2" x WOF inner border strips to sides; trim even with top and bottom. Sew remaining solid green strips to top and bottom; trim even with sides.
Step 4. Sew green print 4 1/2" x LOF outer border strips to sides; trim even with top and bottom. Sew remaining green print strips to top and bottom; trim even with sides.


Assembly Diagram

Quilting and Finishing
Step 5. Mark quilting design.
Step 6. Layer and baste quilt top for quilting method of your choice. Bind wall hanging with remaining green print strips.
